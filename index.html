Who is tklAir9?

My name, Uzair Hasan, is very generic, its the type of name you would find on those naming websites some parents would go on to pick a name for their child. Uzair is the name of a prophet in both Islam and Judaism, which makes it very popular with people in those groups. If you try to google Uzair Hasan, you would find tens of people with the same name. I hated to have a full name that is not unique to you, though I could understand why that is the case. That's where Kelfish4 comes in. I wanted a name that you could google and find the achievements of. I know friends who feel like having a unique name is very annoying, as people could easily find information about you with a single search. Than you see others who take advantage of that, those are the people I became envious of. I chose Kelfish4 as it was the name I use for my in game name. Unfortunately, googling Kelfish4 at the moment would give you a bunch of video game related information (which is all me), but I already created used this name on online profiles and contest, so there is no going back now. Though I do admit, something a bit mature would have been nice.
